Editing Process

Photo editing is a crucial aspect of my work. I always attempt to take many photos to be able to perfectly capture the moment. This approach is often necessary when ensuring that each image reflects the mood and intent of the moment. After making selections with my clients, I make necessary corrections that make the photos feel realistic. If necessary, I will make adjustments like colorgrading to set the mood or theme. Other times I use photoshop skills to enhance details just like an artist, it removes imperfections and satisfies our clients needs.

My Top 5 Photography Tips

  1. Always check your lighting
  2. Make sure to use composition techniques
  3. Keep your lens clean
  4. Experiment with different angles
  5. Don't be afraid to take hundreds of photos
  6. Photo editing is your best friend
